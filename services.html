What We Do

Exterior

Ample insight can be gained from inspecting the exterior. Proper grading and surface water control is vital for the longevity and health of the structure. We look at the conditions of exterior siding, decks and balconies, primary garages or carports, landscaping, grading, elevation, drainage, driveways, sidewalks, fascia, trim, doors, windows, and lights.

Roofing

The roof has an incredibly important mission in the structure and protection of the home. We inspect the roof framing, ventilation, type of roof construction and materials, flashing and roof drainage systems, skylights, chimneys and roof penetrations.

Interior

We understand the interior of the house is what makes the home. We look at a vast number of interior components such as kitchens, bathrooms, walls, ceilings, floors, steps and railings, doors and windows.

Structural

Plumbing

In homes, water can be our biggest nightmare and bankrupter. We make sure the plumbing system is functioning as intended through identifying pipe materials used for the supply, DWV systems, water heaters. We also inspect all fixtures, including toilets, showers, sinks, faucets and taps.

HVAC

We look at everything that works to keeps your home comfortable. We inspect installed heating and cooling equipment, furnaces, boilers, vent and flue systems, ductwork, chimneys and fireplaces.

Electrical

Improperly installed electrical work can pose serious threats of shock and fire hazard. We look at the service drop, cables and raceways, main panels, circuit breakers, distribution wires and grounding, receptacles, ceiling fans and light fixtures, carbon monoxide and smoke detectors.

Attic & Crawlspaces

We’re not afraid to get a little dirty. Crawlspaces and attics are hugely important areas to inspect due to possibly hidden structural, electrical, plumbing and ventilation issues
